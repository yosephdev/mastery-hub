Mentor Rules & Requirements

Learn about the guidelines and expectations for MasteryHub mentors.

Qualifications

  • Minimum of 3 years professional experience in your field
  • Relevant certifications or degrees
  • Excellent communication skills

Code of Conduct

  1. Always maintain professionalism and respect
  2. Protect mentee privacy and confidentiality
  3. Provide honest and constructive feedback
  4. Be punctual and prepared for all sessions
  5. Continuously update your skills and knowledge

Session Guidelines

  • Sessions should be focused and goal-oriented
  • Provide clear explanations and examples
  • Encourage mentee participation and questions
  • Stay within the scheduled time frame
