Mentor Help Center

Find resources and answers to common questions for MasteryHub mentors.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I schedule a session?

    Log into your mentor dashboard and click on "Schedule Session". Choose an available time slot and confirm.

  • What if a mentee doesn't show up?

    Wait for 15 minutes. If the mentee doesn't arrive, mark the session as "No Show" in your dashboard.

  • How do I receive payment?

    Payments are processed every two weeks. Ensure your payment details are up to date in your profile settings.
